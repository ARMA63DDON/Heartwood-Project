About Our Wooden Products

The pictures showing our products are representative examples of what to expect when purchasing our products.

At Heartwood Studios we use "real" wood. Because of this no two similar products will look exactly the same.

Wood Care

Solid woods have a natural beauty but they must be treated differently than plywoods or pressed wood products. It is best to protect wood from bumps and scratches whenever possible. Also, real wood is porous and it breathes as the temperature and moisture content of the air changes. Try to avoid either very damp or very dry storage areas. Also avoid direct sunlight and excessive heat which can cause warping. If warping does occur it can often be corrected by moving the wood product to a less dry area where the wood can absorb moisture.
